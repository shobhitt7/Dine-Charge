About Dine&Charge

Welcome to Dine&Charge, your ultimate companion for electric vehicle travel! We understand the unique needs of EV owners who love to explore, and we've built this platform to make your journeys seamless and enjoyable.

Our mission is simple: to connect you with fantastic restaurants that also offer convenient and reliable DC fast charging stations. No more range anxiety, no more compromising on your dining experience. With Dine&Charge, you can power up your vehicle while savoring a delicious meal, all in one stop.

Key features of Dine&Charge include:

  • Smart route planning to identify optimal charging and dining stops.
  • A curated selection of high-quality restaurants with diverse cuisines.
  • Detailed information on charger types, charging times, and availability.
  • Easy reservation system for both your table and charging spot.

We are passionate about sustainable travel and believe that owning an EV should enhance, not complicate, your adventures. Join our growing community and transform your road trips!
